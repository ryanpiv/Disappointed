Our History

Recently formed guild from a group of friends with over a decade of raiding experience. We have members who have been enjoying the game together since BC/Lich King eras with some members knowing each other from games prior to WoW. Disappointed was created in October 2016.


We are looking for those willing to join our semi-hardcore weekend raid as we quickly move through Heroics and begin Mythic progression. We want folks who like to play with their all and enjoy themselves while doing it.


We support a relaxed environment that balances life in the game and out of it. Our goal is to diverge from the traits found in typical raiding guilds while still achieving the same accomplishments.


Get In Touch

We have a short, 3 step application process.


1. Fill out the Google Form questionnaire (clicky).

2. Have a discussion with a recruitment officer once contacted.

3. Trial in our raids.


Have another question? Our guild members can answer in our absence. Feel free to /who us and message someone online.
